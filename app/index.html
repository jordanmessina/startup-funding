Adding Convertible Notes

Add all outstanding convertible notes. After a convertible note is created, you can add investors to that note.

Adding Founders

Put all your founders / equity here. You can also put your option pool. Do everything in terms of %, not number of shares. For instance, if there are 1,000,000 shares issued, and you own 200,000, put "20" for equity %.

Adding Equity Rounds

Enter all equity rounds' pre-money valuation. After an equity round is created, you can add investors to that round. The first equity round you enter should be the Series A, and that's what the convertible notes will convert at.

Adding Investors

Put all your investor names here. You will add their investments later in the Convertible Notes and Equity Rounds sections.

Equity Breakdown

This is the equity % stake everyone has in the company. Convertible Note investors will not have equity until the first equity round.

Startup Funding

This is a simple tool that demonstrates the mechanics of Convertible Notes and Equity Rounds, and shows how they affect each other.

If you're here to learn about Convertible Notes and Equity Rounds, a great first step is using the interactive tutorial.
